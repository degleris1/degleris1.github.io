Electrical Engineering PhD @ Stanford
Optimization and power systems

I'm currently a 5th year PhD candidate studying optimization and market design for the electricity grid. My focus is on developing computational tools for long-term grid planning problems. I'm grateful to be supported by the Stanford Graduate Fellowship and the DOE Computational Science Graduate Fellowship.
